Terms & Conditions

1. Free Pre-Session

Each student is required to attend a free pre-session before booking any official tutoring sessions.

The pre-session is used to:

  • Assess the student’s current mathematics skills
  • Allow parents/guardians to understand the tutoring approach
  • Decide whether to continue with regular sessions

The free pre-session does not guarantee availability for future sessions.

2. Cancellation Notice

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to be eligible for any refund. Late cancellations or no-shows may be counted as completed sessions.

3. Rescheduling

Rescheduling is subject to availability. Missed sessions without prior notice may not be rescheduled.

4. Payment

Payment must be completed before the scheduled session. Sessions will not be held without confirmed payment.

5. Tutor Discretion

The tutor reserves the right to refuse or discontinue tutoring services if the learning environment is not respectful or cooperative.

In such cases, unused sessions may be refunded according to the refund policy.
